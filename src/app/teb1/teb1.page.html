CNRG | Reporting Application

Centre for Natural Resource Governance

CNRG is implementing a project that seeks to enhance reporting of human rights abuses in selected communities were mining is taking place in Zimbabwe.

The project involves capacitating communities to collect data on human rights abuses and reporting the data in real time.

The project aims to enhance the monitoring of rights, including environmental management in communities through crowdsourcing of evidence of human rights abuses and environmental degradation in the communities.

The goal of the project is to map and track trends of human rights abuses using data and ultimately lobbying extractive companies and the state to uphold rights of communities that host natural resources, based on the evidence gathered.
