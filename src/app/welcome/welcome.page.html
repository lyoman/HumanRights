Centre for Natural Resource Governance

Introduction: Welcome to Centre for Natural Resource Governance mobile platform.

This app will assist you to report incidences or cases of human rights abuses that you observe in your community. These cases or incidences should be related to extractive activities occurring in your area.

The information that you provide will assist CNRG and your community to advocate for justice for those affected. CNRG will treat this information with utmost confidentiality; without disclosing your name(s). It should take you 2 minutes to complete the report. Thank you.
